Pink Floyd
Pink Floyd is a British rock band formed in 1965. The band, known for their iconic music, experimental soundscapes, and psychedelic imagery, revolutionized the music industry and left an indelible mark on generations of fans. With classic albums like "The Dark Side of the Moon," "Wish You Were Here," and "The Wall," Pink Floyd is widely considered one of the greatest, if not the greatest, rock bands of all time.
