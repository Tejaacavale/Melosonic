The Beatles
The Beatles were a revolutionary British rock band that formed in Liverpool in 1960. The group, consisting of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr, became famous for their innovative approach to songwriting, musicianship, and live performances. Their music and cultural impact have endured for over five decades, cementing their status as one of the greatest and most influential bands of all time.
