TOP ARTISTS
  • Pink Floyd
    Formed in London in 1965 and known for their psychedelic and progressive rock sound, elaborate live shows, and concept albums, they became one of the most influential bands of all time with works like "The Wall" and "The Dark Side of the Moon"
  • The Beatles
    Formed in Liverpool in 1960 and consisting of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr, they are one of the most influential and commercially successful bands in the history of music whose impact on popular culture continues to be felt.
  • J. Cole
    Born in Frankfurt, Germany in 1985, he grew up in North Carolina, where he began his music career. Cole is known for his introspective and thought-provoking lyrics. With his storytelling ability and lyrical prowess, he has become one of the most respected and influential artists in hip hop.
  • Kendrick Lamar
    Born in Compton, California in 1987, he first gained attention with his mixtapes. Known for his socially conscious and politically charged lyrics, Lamar has become one of the most important and influential figures in hip hop. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest rappers of all time, with a lasting impact on the genre.
  • Kanye West
    Born in Atlanta, Georgia in 1977, and raised in Chicago, Illinois, he first gained fame for his production work with Jay-Z. Known for his innovative and boundary-pushing music, West has released several critically acclaimed albums. Despite controversy and outspoken behavior, Kanye West remains a hugely influential and respected figure in hip hop and pop culture.
